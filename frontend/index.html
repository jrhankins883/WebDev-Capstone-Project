REELPLAY

For all the movie geeks, film nerds, cinephiles, screen fiends, or anyone that just loves movies.

To simply find an entertainer and see their filmography, click above. Type who you'd like to see in the search bar, click on their picture, and their filmography will appear beside them.

Not in the mood to find a movie on your own? Click above. You'll be given an option to search for one entertainer or pick a genre. Pick a number to determine how many movies you'd like returned, click on "Find A Movie for Me" and a random list of movies will appear. Decide from there which movie you think you'd enjoy the most. If you don't like the selection you can refresh as many times as you'd like.

Want to play a game? Click above. This is the Slot Game. You'll be asked to select a number and based on the number you select, the slot game will find two entertainers who have worked together that many times and a list of their movies will appear. Press the "Push Me" button to start the slots.
